With the rounded number of holes, I used the following equation to calculate frequency
for both methods:
Frequency (Hz) = Angular Speed (rps) * Number of Holes
But the yielded frequencies in both methods were off by 4-8 Hz from the intended frequencies.
The last method I tried was writing a program (Appendix B) that ran through different numbers of
holes and rps numbers to determine whether a given frequency was within 1.5 Hz of the desired
frequency.
I ended up finding that 3 rps was the smallest rps that yielded non-decimal number of
holes and within 1.5 Hz of every desired frequency.
